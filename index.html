Lab Rules & Safety Warnings

  1. Do not feed the fireflies after midnight.
  2. Wear fire-resistant socks when handling glow-dust.
  3. Report any spontaneously sentient objects to Quibble immediately.
  4. Do not attempt to tune a Whisper-Quill with spoons.
  5. If an experiment sings, record it; do not join in unless instructed.

These rules are enforced by polite suggestion and a very loud gong.
